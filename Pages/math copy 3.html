Lesson-5

Examples of percentages are: 10% is equal to 1/10 fraction 20% is equivalent to ⅕ fraction 25% is equivalent to ¼ fraction 50% is equivalent to ½ fraction 75% is equivalent to ¾ fraction 90% is equivalent to 9/10 fraction Percentages have no dimension. Hence it is called a dimensionless number. If we say, 50% of a number, then it means 50 per cent of its whole. Percentages can also be represented in decimal or fraction form, such as 0.6%, 0.25%, etc.
