Lesson-4

The solar system comprises the Sun, eight (formerly nine) planets, over 210 known moons, asteroids, comets, and the interplanetary medium. It is a component of the observable universe, which humans can observe with or without technological aid, while the universe itself may be infinite. Ancient astronomers observed the Sun, Moon, and brightest planets, laying the groundwork for astronomy.

However, our understanding of the cosmos is centered on the solar system and its immediate surroundings, which still mark the furthest extent of our physical exploration and theoretical comprehension.
